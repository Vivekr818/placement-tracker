What's inside

Everything placement season demands.

Six powerful modules. One focused interface. No distractions, no missed deadlines.

🏢
Company Tracker
Add every company you've applied to. Track stage — Applied → OA → Interview → Selected. Filter, sort, and never lose track of a deadline.
Applications
📊
Visual Funnel
See your full application pipeline at a glance — a live funnel chart showing how many are in each stage, plus your selection rate.
Insights
📚
Study Roadmap
Tailored paths for product-based (DSA, System Design) or service-based (Aptitude, Verbal) companies. Week-by-week, topic-by-topic.
Preparation
✅
Daily Tasks
Small, achievable tasks every day — solve 2 problems, revise OS concepts, prep an HR answer. Built to keep you moving without overwhelm.
Consistency
🔥
Streak System
Complete daily tasks to build a streak. A 28-day calendar shows your consistency history. Watch the pattern. Stay accountable to yourself.
Motivation
🌐
Public Profile
Share your preparation journey — stats, badges, consistency — with a public link. Show it to recruiters, friends, or your college network.
